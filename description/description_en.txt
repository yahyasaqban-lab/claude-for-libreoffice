Claude for LibreOffice

Use Claude to analyze, edit, and create spreadsheets. Purpose-built AI integration for LibreOffice Calc.

Features:
- Analyze selected cells and get insights about your data
- Explain complex formulas step by step
- Chat with Claude about your spreadsheet
- Get suggestions for improvements and new formulas

Professionals who work extensively with spreadsheets can use Claude for LibreOffice to collaborate with Claude directly where they work.

Claude for LibreOffice accelerates spreadsheet work through intelligent assistance. It reads complex multi-tab workbooks, explains calculations with cell-level citations, and helps you understand and modify your data.

Whether you're debugging errors, building new models, or analyzing existing data, Claude maintains full transparency by providing clear explanations at every step.

Requirements:
- LibreOffice 7.0 or later
- Python 3.8 or later
- Anthropic API key (get one at https://console.anthropic.com)

Configuration:
Your API key is stored locally in ~/.config/claude-libreoffice/config.json

For support, visit: https://claude.ai
